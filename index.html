A Letter For You

Dear Manal,

Today isn't just another day on the calendar. It's the day someone incredibly beautiful entered this world, and everything since has been a little brighter because of it.

Thank you for existing. Thank you for bringing warmth into rooms that didn't know they needed it, for the kind of laughter that makes people forget their bad day, and for being so effortlessly, wonderfully you.

May every dream you've quietly carried find its way to you. May every tear you've ever cried be repaid with a smile that lasts longer. May your heart stay exactly as full of love as it has always given away.

May happiness choose you — not just today, but every single ordinary Tuesday from here on. May the people around you cherish you the way you deserve, and may you never forget how rare you are.

May Allah bless you with endless peace, good health, quiet success, loud laughter, and a year so full of beautiful memories that you run out of room to keep them all.

Happy birthday, once again. Keep smiling the way only you can.

You deserve the world. ❤️
